Welcome to World of Wonders Toys

Our journey began in 1970 with a dream to bring joy and wonder into the lives of children worldwide. With our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction, we have become a trusted name in the toy industry.

Our Story

In 1970, World of Wonders Toys was founded by John and Lisa Thompson, a dynamic husband-and-wife duo with a shared love for toys and a vision to bring joy to children's lives. Their small toy store in a cozy neighborhood quickly became a favorite destination for children and parents alike.

With the support of our community, we expanded our offerings, designing and curating a wide array of toys that cater to every child's unique interests and developmental needs. Over the years, our passion for play has only grown stronger, and we continue to evolve with the changing times while staying true to our founding principles. Our journey has been marked by countless smiles, giggles, and moments of pure delight. We've seen generations of children grow up with our toys, and each new discovery adventure fuels our commitment to creating magical play experiences.

Mission and Vision

At World of Wonders Toys, our mission is to ignite wonder and foster creativity in children through play. We believe that toys have the power to inspire, educate, and bring joy to young hearts. With every toy we create, we strive to encourage imagination and curiosity, nurturing the seeds of future innovators and dreamers. Our vision is to be the world's most loved and trusted toy brand, known for our innovative designs and commitment to making every childhood magical.

Meet Our Team

Our team is a family of toy enthusiasts who are passionate about crafting memorable play experiences. From our talented designers and engineers to our dedicated customer support team, each member contributes their unique expertise and love for play.

We foster a culture of collaboration, creativity, and fun, which reflects in the toys we create. Our team's dedication and expertise ensure that every toy meets the highest standards of safety, quality, and play value.

Customer-Centric Approach

At World of Wonders Toys, our customers are at the heart of everything we do. We are committed to providing exceptional service and delivering toys that exceed expectations.

We listen to our customers' feedback and use it to continually improve our products. Your satisfaction is our top priority, and we are always here to assist you on your journey to a world of wonder!

Awards and Recognition

Over the years, our dedication to excellence has earned us several industry awards and recognition. These accolades serve as a testament to our unwavering commitment to delivering toys that inspire, educate, and entertain.

Some of our notable awards include:

  • Toy Excellence Award - Best Innovative Toy (2000)
  • Parents' Choice Award - Top Toy of the Year (1997)
  • Toy Industry Association's Toy of the Decade (2010)

We are proud of these achievements, and they inspire us to keep pushing the boundaries of play and designing toys that captivate hearts and minds.

Social Responsibility

As part of our commitment to making a positive impact, we actively support local communities and charitable organizations. We believe in giving back and ensuring that children everywhere have access to the wonder of play.
